FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

This is where we have collectively answered the questions that we frequently and repeated recieve on a daily basis.


Where Can I learn?

Come and learn our next open day or come visit us at NCC Aviation
You can come to our offices and talk about training or we will put you with our staff or members.


How Can I Apply?

Applying for flight training can be done through contacting us.
But we strongly suggest you check out our Training Online section


Can I Do My Pilot Training Part Time?

Students can choose whether to undertake pilot training.
For more information Contact Us


Do I have To pay For All My Training In Advacne?

Training fees are paid in advanced but usually in installments but inlines with your training schedule


What Kind Of Aircraft Can I Fly?

Pilot training is incremental and involves gaining the skills and experience to fly a range of aircraft types, from single through to multi engine, and earning various instrument and operations ratings. Pilots can only fly aircraft appropriate to their licence and rating type.


Are there any health risks in joining the Aviation?

No. If an accident or something happens then NCC aviation has you fully covered.
Accidents are bound to happen, so why let that stop you at a shot of a lovely career
