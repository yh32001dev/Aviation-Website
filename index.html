DJI AIR 2S

The outstanding Air 2S combines the best features of our previous favorite DJI drones, the Mavic Air 2 and Mavic 2 Pro. You get the former's compact, folding design as well as the latter's 20MP 1-Inch sensor. The combination is brilliant, making the Air 2S our top pick for both hobbyists and professionals looking for a small drone that they can take anywhere.While the Mavic Air 2 is still a good choice for those on a tighter budget, the Air 2S offers some useful advantages over its predecessor. DJI Mavic 3

Looking for the most powerful compact drone on the market? It's the DJI Mavic 3. This aerial powerhouse is outfitted with a dual-camera system that combines a large, 20MP Four Thirds sensor with a useful 162mm telephoto lens. And the Mavic 3 packs all of this into a backpack-friendly package that's lighter than the Mavic 2 Pro model it's based on. Other enhancements over its predecessor include a 46-minute battery life (equivalent to about half an hour of actual flight time) and the ability to shoot 5K/50p video or 4K/120p slow-mo footage. Upgrade to the DJI Mavic 3 Cine bundle for 1TB of internal storage, a fancy DJI RC Pro controller, and the ability to shoot video in Apple ProRes 422 HQ format.
